# Whisprompt

A teleprompter for **Even Realities G2** smart glasses. Read your scripts, notes, and speeches on your glasses display — hands-free, eyes up.

## Features
- 📜 Load any text — it shows on your G2 glasses
- ▶️ Auto-scroll at your reading speed (WPM), or scroll manually (finger / touchbar)
- 🎛️ Adjustable speed, column width, brightness, and reading window (wide / narrow)
- 👓 Live "as-on-glasses" preview — drag the frame to scrub position
- 🤙 Control from the glasses touchbar: start, pause, scroll, switch wide/narrow
- 📴 Fully offline — no account, no cloud
- 🌍 7 languages

## Install
1. Download the latest **Whisprompt.apk** from the [Releases](../../releases) page.
2. On your Android phone, allow *Install unknown apps* for your browser/files app, then open the APK and install.
3. Grant Bluetooth + notification permissions and connect to your G2.

**Requires** Android 8.0+ and Even Realities G2 glasses.
