# Whisprompt — Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 16 July 2026

Whisprompt ("the app") is a teleprompter for Even Realities G2 smart glasses. Privacy is the core of the app: **Whisprompt works fully offline and does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.**

## What we collect
**Nothing.** Whisprompt has no accounts, no sign-up, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind. We do not operate servers that receive your data.

## Your content
The scripts and text you load stay **on your device**. They are sent only to your own Even Realities G2 glasses over a direct Bluetooth connection. Your text is never uploaded anywhere and is not accessible to us or any third party.

## Permissions the app uses
- **Bluetooth** — to find and connect to your G2 glasses and display your text. No location data is collected; Bluetooth is used solely for the glasses connection.
- **Notifications / foreground service** — to keep the glasses connection alive while the app runs in the background.

These permissions are used only for the app's core function and for nothing else.

## Premium activation
Optional premium features are unlocked with a license key that you enter in the app. The key is verified **locally on your device** using cryptography; no personal data is sent during activation. License keys are purchased separately through a Telegram bot; that purchase is handled by Telegram and its payment providers under their own terms, and Whisprompt does not receive your payment details.

## Third parties
Whisprompt does not include third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs, and does not sell or share any data, because it does not collect any.

## Children
The app is not directed at children and collects no data from anyone.

## Changes
If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this page with a new effective date.
